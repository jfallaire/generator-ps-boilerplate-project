Welcome to  IOW's documentation!
=======================================

This implementation of work (IOW) supplements Coveo Online Help and Coveo Administrator Training in helping administrators and developers to configure, maintain, debug, customize and install/restore the Coveo implementation performed at  by Coveo Professional Services.
Before reading this guide, it is strongly suggested to get yourself familiar with some sections of the Coveo Online Help:

* `Coveo Cloud V2`_
* `Coveo for Salesforce Online help`_
* `Coveo JavaScript Framework`_
